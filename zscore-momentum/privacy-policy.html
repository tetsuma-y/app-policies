Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Z-Score Momentum ("the App") is an Android application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No account creation is required to use the App.

2. Network Requests

The App connects to the CoinGecko API solely to retrieve publicly available cryptocurrency market data (prices for BTC, ETH, and SOL). No user-identifying information is sent with these requests.

3. Local Data Storage

Market data fetched from the API is cached locally on your device using an on-device database (Room / SQLite). This data never leaves your device and is used only to reduce redundant network calls.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • CoinGecko API — for cryptocurrency price data. CoinGecko's privacy policy can be found at coingecko.com/en/privacy.

5. Analytics & Advertising

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
